Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Coders Gym ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application that helps users practice coding problems. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Data you provide

  • LeetCode username — used to fetch your profile and progress from LeetCode's public API. We do not store your LeetCode password.

Data collected automatically

  • Crash reports — via Firebase Crashlytics, to identify and fix bugs. Includes device model, OS version, and stack traces.
  • Usage analytics — via Firebase Analytics, to understand which features are used. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Advertising data — via Google AdMob, to serve ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to personalize ads. See Google's advertising policies.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your LeetCode profile and progress within the app
  • To improve app stability and fix crashes
  • To understand app usage and improve features
  • To serve advertisements

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config, Cloud Messaging
  • Google AdMob — Advertising
  • LeetCode — Public API for user data

Data Storage

Your preferences and recent activity are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a backend server or database with your personal data.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

You can clear all local app data by uninstalling the app. To request deletion of any analytics data, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@hourproof.app.
