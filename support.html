Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?

On the login screen, tap "Continue with" to sign in with your LeetCode account via a secure webview, or tap "Enter Your UserName" to browse with just a username (no password needed).

Can I use the app without a LeetCode account?

Yes! Tap "Enter Your UserName" and type any public LeetCode username to browse problems, view profiles, and access most features.

How do I write and submit code?

Open any problem and tap the code editor button (orange <> icon). Choose your programming language, write your solution, and tap Run Code or Submit.

What programming languages are supported?

The code editor supports all languages available on LeetCode, including Python, Java, C++, JavaScript, Go, Rust, and more.

How do I change the editor theme?

In the code editor, tap the preferences icon to change themes (Monokai, Dracula, Nord, and more), font size, and tab settings.

Is my data stored on a server?

No. All your preferences, recent questions, and settings are stored entirely on your device. The app communicates directly with LeetCode's servers only to fetch problems and submit code.
